Privacy Policy

Corporate Stalker — LinkedIn Follow-Up Tracker

Corporate Stalker does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All information is stored locally on your device.

What Data Is Stored

When you choose to track a LinkedIn contact, the extension saves the following to your browser's local storage:

  • Contact name, headline, company, and location (as displayed on their LinkedIn profile)
  • LinkedIn profile URL
  • Follow-up dates and status that you set
  • Message templates that you create
  • Your theme preference (dark or light mode)

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively in your browser using the Chrome Extension Storage API (chrome.storage.local). Data never leaves your device.

Data Collection & Transmission

This extension does not:

  • Send data to any external server
  • Use analytics, telemetry, or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Store cookies or use fingerprinting
  • Access your LinkedIn credentials or login session

Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

  • storage — Save tracked contacts and settings locally
  • alarms — Schedule follow-up reminders
  • activeTab / tabs — Detect when you are on a LinkedIn profile page
  • scripting — Read profile information from the current LinkedIn page
  • host_permissions (linkedin.com) — Operate only on LinkedIn pages

Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing its storage through your browser settings. Individual contacts can be archived or removed from within the extension.
